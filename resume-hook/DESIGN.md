## Style Prompt
High-energy TikTok-native career advice. Maximalist Type energy — text IS the visual. Bold, punchy, urgent. Every frame earns attention. Social-first resume hook.

## Colors
- #0F0F0F — deep near-black background
- #F0F0F0 — off-white primary text
- #FFD60A — electric yellow accent (tip numbers, stats, CTA)
- #E63946 — urgent red (hook emphasis, bad examples)
- #22C55E — success green (good examples)

## Typography
- Montserrat Black (900 weight) for all headlines and tip numbers
- Montserrat Bold (700 weight) for body labels and sub-lines

## What NOT to Do
- No gradient text (H.264 banding)
- No soft drop shadows — raw contrast only
- No serif fonts — punchy and fast
- No static decoratives — everything breathes
- No long sentences in captions — max 4 words per group
